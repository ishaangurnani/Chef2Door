Chef Biography

Arnold Williams

Arnold Williams is a Jamaican chef who has gained international recognition for his mastery of Caribbean cuisine. He grew up in Kingston and developed a love for cooking with fresh, tropical ingredients. After studying at culinary school in Jamaica, he worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants across the Caribbean before opening his own establishment, "Arnold's Kitchen." Williams is known for his use of traditional Jamaican spices and flavors, and his dedication to using locally sourced, sustainable ingredients.

Signature Dish: Jamaican Brown Chicken with Callaloo

A traditional Jamaican dish made with chicken that has been marinated and cooked in a rich, flavorful sauce. It is typically served with callaloo, a leafy green vegetable similar to spinach.
