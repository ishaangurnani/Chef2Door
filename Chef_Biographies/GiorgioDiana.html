Chef Biography

Giorgio Diana

Giorgio Diana is an Italian chef who has gained international recognition for his mastery of traditional Italian cuisine. He grew up in Naples and developed a love for cooking with fresh, seasonal ingredients. After studying at culinary school in Italy, he worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe before opening his own establishment, "Giorgio's Kitchen." Diana is known for his dedication to using authentic Italian ingredients and his passion for preserving traditional Italian dishes, such as pasta alla carbonara and margherita pizza.

Signature Dish: Pizza

A dish made with a flatbread crust, tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. It can be customized to suit individual preferences and tastes.
