Chef Biography

Christopher Schaefer

Christopher Schaefer is a German chef who has gained international recognition for his use of molecular gastronomy techniques. He began his culinary journey at a young age, working in his family's restaurant in Berlin. After studying at culinary school in Germany, he worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe before opening his own establishment, "Schaefer's Kitchen." Schaefer is known for his use of liquid nitrogen and other molecular gastronomy techniques to create innovative and unexpected dishes.

Signature Dish: Mediterranean Salad

A salad made with mixed greens, vegetables such as cucumbers and tomatoes, and feta cheese. It is typically dressed with a lemon and olive oil vinaigrette.
