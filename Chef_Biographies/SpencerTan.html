Chef Biography

Spencer Tan

Spencer Tan is a Singaporean chef known for his innovative and contemporary take on traditional Asian cuisine. He began his culinary journey at a young age, working in his family's restaurant before studying at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris. After returning to Singapore, he worked at several Michelin-starred restaurants before opening his own establishment, "Spencer's Kitchen," which quickly became a local favorite. Tan is known for his use of unusual ingredients and flavors, such as durian and salted egg yolk, and his dedication to using locally sourced produce.

Signature Dish: Fillet-O-Fish Salad

A salad made with pieces of crispy fried fish, mixed greens, and a variety of vegetables such as cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions. It is typically served with a dressing of your choice.
