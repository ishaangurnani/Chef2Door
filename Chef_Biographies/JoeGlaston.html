Chef Biography

Joe Glaston

Joe Glaston is an American chef who has gained international recognition for his experimental and avant-garde approach to cooking. He began his culinary journey at a young age, working in his family's restaurant in New York City. After studying at The Culinary Institute of America, he worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants across the United States before opening his own establishment, "Joe's Kitchen." Glaston is known for his use of unusual ingredients and his dedication to pushing culinary boundaries.

Signature Dish: Continetal Stir Fry

A stir-fried dish that typically includes a mix of vegetables such as broccoli, carrots, peppers, and onions, along with meat (such as chicken or beef) or tofu. It is typically seasoned with a mix of soy sauce, ginger, and garlic.
