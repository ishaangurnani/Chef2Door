Chef Biography

Mojmir Siftar

Mojmir Siftar is a Croatian chef who has made a name for himself by preserving traditional Croatian cuisine while adding his own modern twist. He grew up on a farm and developed a love for cooking with fresh ingredients. After working in several Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, he returned to Croatia and opened his own restaurant, "Mojmir's Kitchen." Siftar is known for his dedication to using local, seasonal ingredients and his passion for preserving traditional Croatian dishes, such as peka and brodet.

Signature Dish: Croatian Collard Greens

A salad made with pieces of crispy fried fish, mixed greens, and a variety of vegetables such as cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions. It is typically served with a dressing of your choice.
