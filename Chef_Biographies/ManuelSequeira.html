Chef Biography

Manuel Sequeira

Manuel Sequiera is a Goan chef known for his fusion cuisine that blends traditional Indian flavors with Western cooking techniques. He grew up in a family of chefs and developed a love for cooking at a young age. After studying at culinary school in Goa, he worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe before opening his own establishment, "Manuel's Kitchen." Sequiera is known for his use of local ingredients and his passion for preserving traditional Goan dishes, such as vindaloo and sorpotel.

Signature Dish: Biryani

A fragrant Indian rice dish made with basmati rice, meat (chicken, lamb, or beef), and a variety of spices and seasonings. It is typically served with raita or a side salad.
