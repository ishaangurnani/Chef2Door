Chef Biography

Miguel Braga

Miguel Braga is a Portuguese chef who has gained international recognition for his innovative use of traditional Portuguese ingredients. He began his culinary journey at a young age, working in his family's restaurant in Lisbon. After studying at Le Cordon Bleu in London, he worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe before returning to Portugal to open his own establishment, "Miguel's Table." Braga is known for his use of unusual ingredients, such as barnacles and black pork, and his dedication to using only the freshest, highest quality ingredients. He has been recognized by several prestigious culinary organizations, including the World's 50 Best Restaurants.
