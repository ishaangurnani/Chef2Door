Chef Biography

Alex Pellegerino

Alex Pellegrino is an Italian-American chef who has gained international recognition for his use of classic Italian cooking techniques. He grew up in Naples and developed a love for cooking with fresh, simple ingredients

Signature Dish: Cherry and Goat Cheese Salad

A salad made with mixed greens, fresh cherries, crumbled goat cheese, and a tangy vinaigrette dressing. The sweetness of the cherries pairs perfectly with the tanginess of the goat cheese, making for a delicious and refreshing salad option.
