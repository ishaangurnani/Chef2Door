Chef Biography

Jack Zhang

Jack Zhang is a Chinese-American chef known for his mastery of Sichuan cuisine. He grew up in Chengdu, China, and developed a love for cooking with spicy flavors. After moving to the United States, he worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants before opening his own establishment, "Jack's Kitchen." Zhang is known for his dedication to using authentic Sichuan ingredients and his passion for introducing Americans to the complex and flavorful cuisine of his homeland.

Signature Dish: Thai Curry

A spicy and flavorful dish made with a coconut milk-based sauce, vegetables such as bell peppers and onions, and meat or tofu. It is typically served over rice.
