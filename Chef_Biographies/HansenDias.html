Chef Biography

Hansen Dias

Hansen Dias is an Indian chef who has gained international recognition for his modern and innovative take on traditional Indian cuisine. He grew up in Mumbai and developed a love for cooking at a young age. After studying at culinary school in Mumbai, he worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe before opening his own establishment, "Hansen's Kitchen." Dias is known for his use of unusual spices and flavors, and his dedication to using locally sourced, sustainable ingredients.

Signature Dish: Chicken Salad

A salad made with cooked chicken, mixed greens, and a variety of vegetables such as cucumbers, tomatoes, and red onions. It can be served with a variety of dressings, such as a vinaigrette or creamy dressing.
